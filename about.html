About


Geunhee Lee is an interaction designer, user interface designer, and design researcher currently studying at MIT Department of Urban Studies and Planning. She dreams of ‘Design for the Real World’ in the physical and virtual world by focusing on identifying the system of intentions and behaviors and visualizing the connections between them. She is passionate about exploring the possibilities of daily life in the future, where seamless interaction across physical and virtual space occurs every day.

Design is

composing an epic poem,

for executing a mural,

painting a masterpiece,

writing a concerto.


But the design is

also cleaning and reorganizing
a Real desk drawer,

pulling an impacted tooth,

baking an apple pie,

choosing sides for
a World backlot baseball game,

and educating a child.


Victor Papanek
